Why Stache Caddie?

Never Leave the Bay

Stop walking back to your PC. Stache Caddie puts the GSPro interface right in your pocket. Effortlessly adjust your aim or take a mulligan without breaking your rhythm.

Pinpoint Accuracy

Experience flawless, real-time control. Our ultra-smooth trackpad ensures that every swipe of your thumb translates perfectly on the impact screen.

Strategic Course Views

Take control of your strategy before you swing. Effortlessly trigger tactical drone flyovers to scout hidden bunkers right from the tee box.

Seamless Experience, Anywhere

Whether you're holding a phone in your pocket or have an iPad mounted next to your hitting bay, the UI scales dynamically.

Team Ball Selection

Enable the multiplayer roster (P1-P4) directly above your trackpad on the PLAY screen to quickly switch scramble balls without returning to the PC.

Precision Trackpad

Use the trackpad for full screen navigation. Tap the gear icon to fine-tune pointer speed and scroll sensitivity.

Deep Customization

Unlock the Admin suite to enforce strict Tournament rules, configure commercial Venue routing, adjust Audio/Visual lighting, and instantly switch dynamic Game Themes.

Under the Hood

Deep configuration, dynamic theming, and administrative controls seamlessly built-in.
