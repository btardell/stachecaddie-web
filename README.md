---

## 🎯 What is this?

Stache Caddie bridges the gap between your physical hitting bay and the GSPro simulation engine. By running a lightweight, invisible background daemon on your PC, you can use our dynamic mobile app (on your iOS/Android smartphone or iPad) as a hyper-responsive trackpad.

Our ecosystem allows you to:
* **Never Leave the Bay:** Natively adjust aim, zoom, and navigate the GSPro menus straight from your pocket.
* **Drone Flyovers:** Execute tactical, hotkey-bound visual scouting directly from the tee-box.
* **Pinpoint Accuracy:** Enjoy 1:1, unthrottled zero-latency swipe tracking. 
* **Team Synergy:** Immediately switch scramble balls and player profiles without needing to reach for a mouse.

## 🛠 Support & Bug Tracking

Because Stache Caddie interfaces directly with complex Windows networking architectures and native input queuing, extreme edge-cases can happen. 

**This repository acts as the central hub for the Stache Caddie community.**

If you experience a crash, find a bug, or simply want to request a new feature:
1. Check the existing **Issues** tab above to see if it's a known obstacle.
2. If it's new, hit **[Report a Bug](https://github.com/btardell/stachecaddie-support/issues/new)** and give us as much detail as you can (Screenshots, OS Version, GSPro Version). 

Your technical feedback is exactly what keeps this platform running flawlessly for everyone!
